除工作流引擎外，还需要这些业务表

业务表概览（7张表）

users 用户表
id, name, email, password
role (admin/operator/customer)
created_at, updated_at
categories 分类表
id, name, slug, parent_id
created_at
products 商品表
id, name, description, price
stock, images(JSON), category_id
status (draft/pending/published)
created_at
orders 订单表
id, order_no, user_id
total_amount, address(JSON)
status (由工作流引擎管理)
created_at
order_items 订单明细
id, order_id, product_id
quantity, unit_price
snapshot(JSON) 商品快照
refunds 退款表
id, order_id, user_id
reason, amount, status
(由工作流引擎管理)
